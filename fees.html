Given the variety and scope of services EECC provides, fees are negotiated based on various factors, including the type, quality and size of the material we will work on. For most projects, we require 50 percent of the fee upfront. The remaining is to be paid at completion of the project. For manuscripts, payment may be split into up to four installments.

It is our professional commitment to work with our clients to ensure that they are satisfied with our work. However, it is not always possible to satisfy individual preferences. Once we agree on terms, the client will sign a contract to that effect.
